Photography

Here is where all of my favorite recent photo work is here for you to take a look at. I've enjoyed photography for a while now, but I've recently got access to a much higher-quality camera and have been having fun learning about the fundementals of photography.

Splatter

This was an photo I took that I felt showed off a great use of contrast. I love taking pictures of nature and finding ways to abstract it fun ways, and I felt this was the best representation of that. To me, this looks like some kind of abstract painting with splatters of block flowing upward. I also just think its neat.

Power Plant

I really enjoyed the compostion of this photo. The symmetry of elements I feel really make for a fun dynamic of the natural trees to the manufactured powerline. I also like the little leading lines from the powerline. They are cool.

Calm in the Storm

I took this photo on a bike ride out in the woods. This was really a in the moment sort of shot, it was just about to be too dark out but I managed to get this cool framing shot.

Criss Cross

This one's pretty neat. This is a picture of a bridge at a bit of a strange angle with a longer lens. This gave it a really nice flattening effect to the image while keeping the lines interesting.

Good Fellow

This one is a just a picture of my dog. He loves to run around like a maniac so getting picture of him was a difficult task. I really liked that I was able to get it framed up just right and was able to get the background blurred. If my dog ever had a LinkedIn, this would probably be his picure.

I'd buy that for a dollar.
